Does it make a lot of noise?
The Phoenix produces acoustic waves by means of high energy collisions between mechanical components within the device, fifteen times per second. As a result, there is significant mechanical noise generated during operation. While steps have been taken to insulate and isolate the noise making components, The Phoenix is still fairly noisy during use and we recommend taking precautions to protect your hearing. For example, we recommend against using The Phoenix in a small, enclosed, acoustically ‘hard’ space such as a tiled bathroom, where sound waves are reflected off of hard surfaces and reinforce one another, effectively making them louder. We suggest using The Phoenix in a larger space which is acoustically ‘softer’ (fewer hard, sound reflecting surfaces—for example a room with carpet and curtains is preferable).

Product Use

Do I need to leave The Phoenix plugged in during the 36 hour lockout?
The device does not need to be plugged in during the 36 hour lockout period. It does not harm the unit if you leave it plugged in, but it has an internal battery which keeps track of timer and usage history and functions so it will countdown the lockout period whether plugged in or not
How often do I reapply Heat Shield on the tip of the Phoenix during use?
We recommend the use of Heat Shield on the tip of the Phoenix on an as needed basis. This means apply it prior to the first pass and re-apply when you notice the trip dragging
How do I plug in The Phoenix?
The Phoenix is NOT a cordless, battery powered device. It must be plugged in for power to operate. The Phoenix doesn’t plug directly into the wall. It is provided with a plug-in transformer which converts household electricity into a low voltage direct current power which is what the Phoenix requires to operate. The Phoenix must be plugged in the entire time you use it. Once a treatment is completed, you may unplug the unit. First unplug the transformer from the wall receptacle, then unplug the power cord from the Phoenix. The Phoenix has an internal battery so it is able to keep track of and remember all of the relevant data—how many treatments you have performed, how much time is left on the ‘lockout’ period, etc. If you have to stop in the middle of a treatment (interrupt the treatment) and unplug the unit, The Phoenix remembers where you left off and will start at the same place when you power it up again.
Why is my Phoenix blinking red lights and displaying a strange number?
One of the safety features built into the Phoenix is a ‘lockout’ period which prevents you from using the Phoenix within 36 hours from the time you completed your last treatment. The Phoenix communicates to you that it is in this ‘lockout’ mode by flashing all 5 of the position indicator lights red while displaying a number in the alphanumeric display window. This number will initially be 36, indicating that you have 36 more hours to wait before you can use The Phoenix again; and the number will decrement by 1 with each passing hour until it gets to 0 at which point you may use The Phoenix for another treatment. The purpose of the ‘lockout’ feature is to prevent over-treating and to give the tissue of the penis time to heal and recuperate. If you treat more frequently, you will not see good results because the tissue never has a chance to heal.
